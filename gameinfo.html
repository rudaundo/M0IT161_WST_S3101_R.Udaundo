ZeroScope Game Info

About the Game

Zero Scope is an intense first-person shooter developed by Jae Games Studio. Step into the role of an elite marksman in a world where precision means survival. Every shot counts — one mistake can be the difference between victory and defeat. Whether you’re playing solo or battling other players online, Zero Scope will test your aim, reflexes, and strategy like never before.

Game Play

Experience fast-paced, tactical combat in immersive 3D environments. Each match challenges you to think quickly, move strategically, and master your weapon to outplay your opponents. Use your surroundings for cover, line up the perfect shot, and prove your skills on the leaderboard.
